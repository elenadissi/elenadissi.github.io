03. Research

Understand Our User.

Extensive interviews were conducted with actively engaged business clients to identify and understand the major pain points that required attention and resolution.
15 +
In-depth interviews were conducted.
Key Findings
The primary concern expressed by the respondents was the inability to sign payments through a mobile device while traveling, on business trips, etc., as there needed access to a computer for signature purposes.
During the interviews, it was revealed that there was a strong desire to have the ability to change currency directly from their mobile devices.
Users shared their concerns about excessive and unnecessary features that were causing an overload in the current mobile application.
Having access to all accounts on mobile devices is essential for individuals who manage multiple accounts.
Users would greatly appreciate the ability to manage deposits within the application.
