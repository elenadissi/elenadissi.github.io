03. Research

Understand Our User.

Questions were crafted based on the problems that users face when they contact the bank or use their products. Thus, we tried to find out the goals and pains of users when they use banking products.
20 +
In-depth interviews were conducted.
Key Findings
Most participants expressed their dislike for waiting in lines at the bank branch and their aversion towards bureaucratic procedures.
They desire to minimize the time spent on bill payments and transfers.
They prefer using credit cards, but the associated costs are deemed expensive.
Users shared their concerns about numerous unnecessary features in banking apps.
Many participants expressed concerns regarding the security of using online banking apps.
Users expressed strong dissatisfaction with the support services provided by banks, citing their inability to address issues effectively, get deeply into problems, and resolve them individually.

05. Wireframes

Created visual representations that outlined the app's structure, layout, and functionality. Wireframes helped us to provide a clear framework for stakeholders to visualize and understand the user interface, enabling effective collaboration, feedback, and refinement throughout the design process.
06. Visual Design

Crafting Intuitive Experiences with Creative Precision.

By carefully considering user needs and behaviors, UI design was created that facilitates efficient navigation, clear communication, and delightful interactions.

Onboarding.

Hassle-free onboarding through easy-to-navigate prompts that allow the user to register quickly without visiting the bank.

Cashback.

An intuitive and straightforward interface for selecting and using cashback benefits.

Payments.

The payment section has been meticulously designed to facilitate quick payments through pre-defined templates and effortless card-to-card transfers in just two clicks.

Understandable Design Kit.

It comprises a comprehensive range of UI components and design elements that are effortless to comprehend and employ. It streamlines the design process and aids developers in expediting development tasks.
